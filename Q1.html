Question 1 = For the multimedia system Linda, give an outline of what it is, its purpose and how it can be defined as a multimedia system

About

Lynda is a growing online learning platform that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals. Their purpose can be stated through their mission statement “To help you learn the skills you need to achieve your full potential.” Multimedia systems are information systems that combine a variety of different media types, including text, numbers, hyperlinks, audio images and video. Lynda can be defined as a multimedia system as the website has all over these various forms of media to help the user learn from their website.
