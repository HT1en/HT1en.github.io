Question 7= Facebook are developing a virtual reality headset called Oculus Rift. Describe what Oculus Rift is and outline its purpose.

About

Oculus Rift is a virtual reality (VR) technology that is manufactured by Oculus VR. The Oculus is a head mounted device that allows users to naturally interact with 3D virtual environments. Although it was released at the end of March of 2016, it has quickly become one of the most popular consumer VR technologies. The simulation varies depending on what area of speciality you are coming from. Simulation can be made to replicate realistic worlds or a completely fantasy world. Computer-generated imagery and content aim at simulating a real presence through senses.
